Past Events


Spring Concert: BRAVE

April 24, 2016

The spring concert is a culmination of a year of hard work, and it also serves as a belated celebration of Christ's Resurrection. This year, the theme of the concert was BRAVE, and the centerpiece was a short film based on the life of Esther.

Concert footage, including the skit in its entirety, will soon be available on the Deliverance Facebook page.

Cookies and Carols

December 3, 2015

Cookies and Carols is an annual concert at which we perform our entire fall repertoire in addition to several traditional Christmas carols. Cookies and milk are provided, and the audience is welcome to sing along as we celebrate the birth of our Savior!
