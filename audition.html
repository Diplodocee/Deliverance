Audition for Deliverance

Deliverance auditions are open to any Washington University student. We hold official audition times at the beginning of each semester, but we are willing to take on new members at any time. If you are interested in joining the group, simply fill out the form at the bottom of the page or send an e-mail to wudeliverance@gmail.com.

During the semester, auditions can happern during our Monday and Saturday rehearsals. Submit the form or send us an e-mail with the date you would like to audition, and we will e-mail you back with the specifics of the audition schedule.

For your audition, please prepare a 30-second selection from a song of your choice in a mainstream style (use your normal singing voice - no rap or heavily stylized country music). The goal of the audition is to make sure that you have a good sense of pitch and rhythm and will be able to learn songs quickly and sing in a way that fits in a group environment. Auditionees are asked to copy pitches and pitch sequences from a piano, to sing their pre-prepared song segments, and to learn and sing a brief segment of a Deliverance arrangement (this does not need to be prepared beforehand - we will have music for you). For examples of songs that Deliverance has done in the past, see Music.
