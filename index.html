Welcome to the Deliverance homepage!

Deliverance is a group of musicians on the campus of Washington University in St. Louis who seek to bring glory to God by praising His name and celebrating the rich history of Christian music.

This site is an introduction to who we are and to what God has done through us. Here, you can learn more about the group and its members, learn about upcoming concerts, and even browse a library of all of our past songs and arrangements, so that you can enjoy them and use them to praise our King anywhere, anytime.

If what you find excites you and you want to join us, you can find out how on the Audition page!
