About Deliverance

Deliverance was started in the fall of 2004 by Christine Wang (WU ’05) in pursuit of her dream of using a capella music to reach the Washington University campus with the Gospel. Over the years, the group's mission has grown from simply being the voice of Christianity within the a capella community toward the goal of being a unifying force and a voice of outreach for the Christians at Wash U. We believe there is a power in music to unify and to express, and we believe that God can work through us and our songs to bring glory to His name and to bring hope and love to those around us. We find in our music a way to praise our God and express His goodness by creating beauty and enjoyment in His name.
