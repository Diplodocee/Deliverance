Chris Munley

A Capella Advisory Committee (ACAC) Representative

Chris Munley is a senior (Class of 2016) from Charlotte, North Carolina. When he's not jamming out on the bass line in an a capella arrangement, you can usually find him in the Phsyics library working on his Physics and Math majors or acting on stage as part of the student theatre organization Cast n' Crew. Chris has a twin brother (not identical) who also attends Wash U. Chris is Deliverance's ACAC representative, which means that he is responsible for meeting with members of other a capella groups to plan what's next for the music community at Wash U.

Vincent Truong

Vincent Truong is a senior (class of 2016) from Vietnam. He sings bass/baritone and is also Deliverance's primary vocal percussionist (beat-boxer). Vincent is passionate about music, movies, and all forms of entertainment, and creates short films with a student organization on campus. Vincent was one of Deliverence's music directors from Fall 2014 to Fall 2015.

Katie Engsberg

General Coordinator

Katie Engsberg is a senior (Class of 2016) studying for a major in the Interdisciplinary Project in the Humanities. She has a great passion for creative writing, and when not pursuing world peace through inter-religious dialogue (which, let’s face it, takes up lots of her time), she enjoys immersing herself in her imagination through reading, writing, watching British television.

F. Scott Fitzgerald, Sherlock, J.R.R. Tolkein, C.S. Lewis, Doctor Who, and the Marvel Cinematic Universe are some of her favorite distractions. She can also draw sporadically and loves baking. As the General Coordinator, Katie looks after the practical functioning of the group: planning rehearsals and events, sending out e-mails, and reserving spaces and equipment.

Laura Zimmerman

Music Director

Deliverance is a group of musicians on the campus of Washington University in St. Louis who seek to glorify God by praising His name and celebrating the rich history of Christian music, including Contemporary Christian, hymns, worship music, and gospel.

Deliverance was started in the fall of 2004 by Christine Wang (WU ’05) as a result of her dream of using a cappella as a witness for the Gospel. Although we are a young group, we hope that our music and our passion will serve to reveal God’s love to the Washington University campus.
