Chris Munley

A Capella Advisory Committee (ACAC) Representative

Deliverance is a group of musicians on the campus of Washington University in St. Louis who seek to glorify God by praising His name and celebrating the rich history of Christian music, including Contemporary Christian, hymns, worship music, and gospel.

Deliverance was started in the fall of 2004 by Christine Wang (WU ’05) as a result of her dream of using a cappella as a witness for the Gospel. Although we are a young group, we hope that our music and our passion will serve to reveal God’s love to the Washington University campus.
