Chris Munley

A Capella Advisory Committee (ACAC) Representative

Chris Munley is a senior (Class of 2016) from Charlotte, North Carolina. When he's not jamming out on the bass line in an a capella arrangement, you can usually find him in the Phsyics library working on his Physics and Math majors or acting on stage as part of the student theatre organization Cast n' Crew. Chris has a twin brother (not identical) who also attends Wash U. Chris is Deliverance's ACAC representative, which means that he is responsible for meeting with members of other a capella groups to plan what's next for the music community at Wash U.

Vincent Truong

Vincent Truong is a senior (class of 2016) from Vietnam. He sings bass/baritone and is also Deliverance's primary vocal percussionist (beat-boxer). Vincent is passionate about music, movies, and all forms of entertainment, and creates short films with a student organization on campus. Vincent was one of Deliverence's music directors from Fall 2014 to Fall 2015.

Katie Engsberg

General Coordinator

Katie Engsberg is a senior (Class of 2016) from Eureka, Missouri. Katie is studying for a major in the Interdisciplinary Project in the Humanities. She has a great passion for creative writing, and when not pursuing world peace through inter-religious dialogue (which, let’s face it, takes up lots of her time), she enjoys immersing herself in her imagination through reading, writing, and watching British television. F. Scott Fitzgerald, Sherlock, J.R.R. Tolkein, C.S. Lewis, Doctor Who, and the Marvel Cinematic Universe are some of her favorite distractions. She can also draw sporadically and loves baking.

In Deliverance, Katie is an alto and the General Coordinator, which means that she looks after the practical functioning of the group: planning rehearsals and events, sending out e-mails, and reserving spaces and equipment.

Laura Zimmermann

Music Director

Laura Zimmermann is a graduating senior (class of 2016) from Illinois. Laura is active on the Mock Trial team and recently was part of the group that led the team to 5th place at nationals. Laura is a soprano and one of Deliverance's two current music directors, which means that she leads Monday rehearsals and plays a role in planning rehearsals and events.

James Norlin

Treasurer

Leah Yuan

Leah Yuan is a junior (Class of 2017) from Mason, Ohio. When she is not at BD studying for her pre-med required classes, she spends her time dancing in WUHHU (WashU’s Hip Hop Union), practicing taekwondo in LNYF’s martial arts team, and of course, singing soprano with Deliverance. Leah loves spending hours jamming on her keyboard or guitar, especially when there are big papers or exams coming up. No matter how busy she is, Leah always enjoys sitting and talking with people about anything and everything. This semester, Leah is abroad studying in London, but she will return for next school year.

Victoria Rabuse

Victoria Rabuse is a sophomore (class of 2018) from Mendota Heights, Minnesota. Lincoln Ward

Music Director

Lincoln is a sophomore (class of 2018) from Castle Rock, Colorado. He is majoring in Earth and Planetary Science with minors in Art and Physics. He loves any kind of science, the arts, building stuff out of random materials – just about anything that involves exploring ideas or being creative. Lincoln really likes music (obviously), the outdoors, animation, cheese, Narnia, superheroes, modeling clay, glue guns, and rocks.
